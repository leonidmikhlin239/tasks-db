$=\cfrac{1}{\sqrt{(2+\sqrt{3})^2}}+\cfrac{1}{\sqrt{(2-\sqrt{3})^2}}=\cfrac{1}{2+\sqrt{3}}+\cfrac{1}{2-\sqrt{3}}=
\cfrac{2-\sqrt{3}+2+\sqrt{3}}{4-3}=4.$\\
