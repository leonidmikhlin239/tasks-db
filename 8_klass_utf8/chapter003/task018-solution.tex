18. $|3x+8|=x\Leftrightarrow \begin{cases}
  \begin{gathered}
    \left[
      \begin{gathered}
        3x+8=x, \hfill
        \\
        3x+8=-x. \hfill
        \\
      \end{gathered}
    \right. \hfill
    \\
    x\geqslant 0.    \\
  \end{gathered}
\end{cases}\Leftrightarrow \begin{cases}
  \begin{gathered}
    \left[
      \begin{gathered}
        x=-4, \hfill
        \\
        x=-2. \hfill
        \\
      \end{gathered}
    \right. \hfill
    \\
    x\geqslant 0.    \\
  \end{gathered}
\end{cases}
\Leftrightarrow x \in \{\varnothing\}.$\\
