(a^2+b^2)\cdot\left(\cfrac{a+b+a-b}{a^2-b^2}\right)^{2}=(a^2+b^2)\cfrac{4a^2}{(a^2-b^2)^2}=\cfrac{4a^2(a^2+b^2)}{(a^2-b^2)^2}=\cfrac{4\sqrt{x}(\sqrt{x}+\sqrt{y})}{(\sqrt{x}-\sqrt{y})^2}.$\\
