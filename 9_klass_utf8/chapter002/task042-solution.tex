\left(\cfrac{x^3+y^3}{x+y}-xy\right):(x^2-y^2)+\cfrac{2y}{x+y}=
\left(x^2-xy+y^2-xy\right):((x-y)(x+y))+\cfrac{2y}{x+y}=(x-y)^2:((x-y)(x+y))+\cfrac{2y}{x+y}=\cfrac{x-y+2y}{x+y}=1.$\\
